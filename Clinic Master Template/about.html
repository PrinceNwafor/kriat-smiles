Our Story

A Clinic Built Around You

Meridian Health Clinic opened its doors in 2011 with three doctors and a belief that patients deserved more time, more clarity, and more compassion than the typical rushed appointment allowed.

Today we're a full multi-specialty clinic with over 40 providers — but that founding belief hasn't changed. Every decision we make, from how we schedule to how we design our waiting rooms, starts with the question: what's best for the patient?

We invest in modern technology not for its own sake, but because it lets our doctors spend less time on paperwork and more time with you.
